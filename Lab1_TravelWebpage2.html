Karl Theodor bridge

Karl Theodor bridge, commonly known as the Old Bridge,is an arch bridge that crosses the Neckar river.
It connects the Old city with the eastern part of the Neuenheim district.
The bridge is well worth strolling along for its views of Heidelberg and its sister bridge, the twin towered Bruckentor.

Heidelberg Zoo

The Heidelberg Zoo is certainly worth a visit, particularly if traveling with youngsters.
On the north bank of the Neckar, this zoological park has more than 1,100 animals encompassing some 250 species.
